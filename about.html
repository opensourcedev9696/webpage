About Us

Welcome to NSS Higher Secondary School Adoor

NSS Higher Secondary school started in 1934 at Adoor, paruthippara campus got affiliated to State syllabus in1998. The school has incredible achievements in academic field.The school stands for excellence in academic and co-curricular activities of the students. It is a community of students, parents and teachers who live in an atmosphere of a family loving and respecting each other. It provides enrichment programs to the high achievers, special care and coaching to the weak students and offer counseling facilities to all the students.

Nair Service Society

The Nair service society, a synonym for selfless service and social commitment was founded by the late Sri. Mannathu Padmanabhan on 31/10/1914. This day is being observed to commemorate the formation of Community organisation on October and the day is known as "NSS Flag Day".
